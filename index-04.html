More About Us

  • What is IHMCL?
  • What we Do?

NHAI jointly with its Concessionaires and Financial Institutions incorporated Indian Highways Management Company Limited (IHMCL) on 26.12.2012 under the Companies Act, 1956 to carryout Electronic Tolling and other ancillary projects of NHAI.

Know More

Presently, IHMCL is implementing Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) on National Highways in India using Passive RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) technology.

Traffic Data Sharing Schemes
